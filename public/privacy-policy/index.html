Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and why.

Our Core Principle: Client-Side Processing

PWA Maker is designed to be a privacy-first, client-side tool. This means all the processing—generating files, handling your app icons, and creating the PWA package—happens directly in your web browser on your own device. We believe this is the most private and secure approach.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the information you enter into the generator. This includes:

  • The website URLs you wish to wrap.
  • Your App Name, Short Name, or Description.
  • Your color selections or other configuration choices.
  • The app icons you upload. Your icon is processed in your browser and is never sent to our servers.

All generated files are created on-the-fly in your browser and are made available for you to download directly in a single package.

Important Disclaimer

While PWA Maker does not collect your data, the PWA you create will load a third-party website. Your interaction with the content inside the generated PWA is governed by the privacy policy and data collection practices of that specific website. PWA Maker has no control over, and assumes no responsibility for, the content or privacy practices of any third-party sites or services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
