About PWA Maker

Empowering anyone to create app-like experiences from the websites they love.

Our Mission

PWA Maker was created to bridge the gap between the web and native apps. Our mission is to provide an intuitive, powerful tool that lets you take any website and wrap it into an installable Progressive Web App. This allows for a focused, full-screen experience without browser tabs and address bars, making your favorite websites feel like native applications on your desktop or mobile device.

Why PWA Maker?

We handle all the technical details—generating the manifest, creating a service worker for basic offline capabilities, and resizing icons—so you don't have to. Simply provide a URL, customize the app's appearance, and download a complete PWA package ready for deployment. It's the fastest way to create a dedicated, installable app for a news site, social media platform, internal company tool, or any other website you use frequently.

Privacy First and Free Forever

This tool is completely free to use and respects your privacy. All processing happens directly in your browser. The URLs you enter and the icons you upload are never sent to our servers. We believe in the power of an open and accessible web, and PWA Maker is our contribution to making it even more versatile.
